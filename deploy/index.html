TaskFlow Dashboard

Project management dashboard with Kanban boards, calendar, analytics, team collaboration, and CSV export.

Kanban Board - Drag-and-drop task management
Calendar - Monthly view with due dates
Analytics - Charts and progress tracking
Team - Member management and workload
Auth - Login/register with roles
CSV Export - Download task data
